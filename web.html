About Us

Road Help Systems, Kenya, is your one-stop garage for all auto repair and maintenance. We have provided the very best automobile service in Kenya since June 2019! At our community owned and operated care center, we believe our work is a reflection of our character. We always strive to uphold our reputation as a personable and reliable service. With our factory-grade diagnostic tools and honest answers provided by our knowledgeable technicians, Road Help Systmes is your cost-effective alternative to dealerships.

Why us

Providing the best auto repair in over 10 locations in Kenya and 24 hours service, means that our technicians understand that you want to preserve the quality of your car as much as possible. Properly maintaining your vehicle will help you prevent complicated repairs and damage in the future. We offer a variety of services to keep your car looking and running like new. If you are in need of general auto service, our expert technicians fix all makes and models of vehicles. So whether you need a complicated brake repair or just an oil change, you can count on the experts at Road Help Systems to get you back on the road quickly and efficiently!
